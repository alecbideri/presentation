Executive Summary

Rwanda's athletes are national assets. Ganza — developed by AJA TECH in partnership with ATRDA — is a purpose-built digital infrastructure that transforms how the sports ecosystem identifies, develops, protects, and monetizes sporting talent.

It is a national operating system for sports — a sovereign platform that turns every registered athlete into a verified, trackable, and exportable digital asset.

Ganza fulfills strategic goals by replacing fragmented systems with a unified "Digital Backbone" that attracts investment through data-verified performance.
