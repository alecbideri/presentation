Executive Summary

Rwanda's athletes are national assets. Ganza is a purpose-built digital infrastructure that transforms how Rwanda identifies, develops, protects, and monetizes its sporting talent.

It is a national operating system for sports — a sovereign platform that turns every registered athlete into a verified, trackable, and exportable digital asset.

Ganza fulfills Vision 2050 goals by replacing fragmented spreadsheets with a "Digital Backbone" that attracts foreign currency through data-verified transfers.
